E7 Speed Calculator

Instructions:

  1. Input your units data in the green boxes. Each row is one hero
  2. Input the CR of the opponents in the red boxes
  3. Boxes under the unit column is for note-taking purposes
  4. Press Add Unit to add more of your units (max 4). Press Add Opponent Unit to add one of theirs (max 4)
  5. Press Calculate! to see the calculated speed ranges in the Opponent's speed boxes

Numbers are approximate using calculations explained in Discord.
Use units with CR closer to 100% for accurate ranges.
